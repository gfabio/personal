The Rite

Note: in-browser play requires Unity Web Player, which does not run in Chrome.

I developed The Rite by myself in Unity 3D in less than 48 hours for the Global GameJam 2016. The theme was "ritual". Assets were created with Maya 2015, GIMP, Paint Tool SAI, and Garageband. A couple friends provided images to use in the game; credits are on the GameJame site, linked below.

Aster

I came up with the concept for Aster in March 2015, while studying abroad in Singapore at Nanyang Technological University. I developed the idea there as a final project for my History of Games class, and brought it back to University of Rochester, where it was chosen as a senior capstone project.

I am the Project Manager, Creative Director, Artist, Level Designer, Game Writer, Technical Writer, and Character Modeler on the team (and various other positions as needed). The game is built in Unreal Engine. I designed levels and drew concept art and visual assets in Paint Tool SAI and GIMP; I modeled characters in Sculptris and Maya 2015. Some art can be found here. I wrote the project plan and game script and have presented the game formally to panel members half a dozen times. As the team leader I have also kept my team organized and on track. Aster is due to be released in April 2016.
